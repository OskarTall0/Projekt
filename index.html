Partial comeback to powerlifting

After becoming the national champion in november 2023, I took a break from competing and training for a while to regain my mental health since I was feeling burned out from not having any free time.

October 15, 2024

Estonian national champion

I achieved what I once thought was impossible. During the competition, I set a new personal record by benching 181.5 kg, which earned me first place in the Juniors 120+ category and third overall among juniors. This performance crowned me as the Estonian National Champion in Juniors 120+ and also the raw bench press record holder for the same class.

November 11, 2023

First ever competition in powerlifting

The day that gave the motivation to begin my semi-professional career in powerlifting. During this competition i achieved 135 Kg bench press.
